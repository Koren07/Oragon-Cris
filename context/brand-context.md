# Brand Context

**Company:** Oragon  
**Mission:** To be the definitive, absolute best B2B platform for Apple Resellers in Argentina.  

## Visual Identity
Oragon's visual language is heavily inspired by Apple's own design principles to resonate with resellers.
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimalist, flat "Bento-box" UI design.
- **Backgrounds:** Strictly plain white (`#ffffff`) or extremely faint gray off-whites (`rgba(250,250,250,0.5)`). *Never* use harsh gradients or heavy colors as backgrounds.
- **Shadows & Borders:** Ultra-subtle. Drop shadows shouldn't exceed `0 4px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.03)`. Borders are thin and light, e.g., `1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`.
- **Typography:** Modern, sans-serif (Inter/San Francisco). Use uppercase with high tracking for small labels (e.g., `PRECIO DE VENTA`).
- **Iconography:** Strictly use **Material Symbols**. No generic emojis or vector icons. 
- **Colors:** Space Black (`#1d1d1f`), Apple Blue (`#0071e3`), and subtle interface shades like gray (`#86868b`).

## Core Message
We empower Apple resellers to stop doing things manually. We provide them with automated tools, synced inventory, and 24/7 AI agents that do the selling for them while they sleep. We are elegant, robust, and highly technical yet incredibly simple to use.
