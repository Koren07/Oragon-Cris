# Product Offering

Oragon is a highly integrated SaaS for Apple Resellers.

## 1. Automated AI Chat Agents
- Connects directly to WhatsApp.
- Reads real-time stock levels.
- Replies instantly 24/7.
- Sends payment links and closes sales independently.

## 2. Real-Time Inventory Management
- Centralized database of stock (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro, 256GB, Titanio Negro).
- Auto-updates pricing based on USD fluctuations ("Actualizado en tiempo real").

## 3. Seamless Migration
- Merchants can upload an old Excel file, and the AI parses it and converts it into structured database items instantly.
- Voice-memo stock updating: Owners can literally send an audio message (e.g., "Agregá 3 fundas de iPhone 14 a 50 lucas") and the inventory updates automatically.

## 4. Advanced Analytics Dashboard
- Clean, Apple-style UI.
- Tracks metrics like active chats, gross margin, closed sales, and lead conversion utilizing high-end Recharts components.
