# Growth Marketing Strategies

Our marketing campaigns rely on showcasing **results** and **automation**. The focus is on saving the reseller time and increasing their sales throughput effortlessly.

## Key Angles
1. **The "While You Sleep" Angle**
   - Focus: 24/7 AI Chat Agents.
   - Narrative: Show a WhatsApp notification at 4:12 AM where the AI successfully reserves an iPhone and sends a payment link without the owner lifting a finger.

2. **The "Death of Excel" Angle**
   - Focus: Painless migration of inventory.
   - Narrative: Resellers hate managing stock manually. Emphasize that Oragon's AI instantly migraes huge Excel spreadsheets to an elegant, real-time dashboard.

3. **The "Ultimate CRM" Angle**
   - Focus: Having everything in one place.
   - Narrative: Integrate real screenshots of the SaaS dashboard, showing messages, leads, and analytics on one beautiful screen. Fast-paced visuals prove it's a real, robust product.
