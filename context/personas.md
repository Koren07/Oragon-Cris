# Buyer Personas — Oragon

Three archetypes of Apple resellers in Argentina. All agents should reference these when generating targeted content.

---

## Persona 1: "El Cuentapropista" — Lucas

**Role:** Solo reseller, self-employed
**Size:** 1-2 people (often just himself + a friend/partner)
**Location:** Home office or small rented space, CABA / GBA
**Age:** 24-35
**Channel:** Sells primarily via WhatsApp and Instagram DMs. Sometimes MercadoLibre.

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Manages stock in a WhatsApp group, a notes app, or a messy Excel sheet
- Responds to customer inquiries manually, sometimes at midnight
- Misses sales because he's asleep or busy when a buyer messages
- Loses track of which units are reserved vs. available
- Prices products manually by checking the dollar exchange rate every morning

### Pain Points
- "Se me pierden los pedidos entre tantos chats"
- "Me pasé la noche respondiendo por WhatsApp y al final no cerré nada"
- "No sé cuántas unidades me quedan de cada cosa"
- "Si no estoy yo, no hay negocio"

### Trigger to Buy Oragon
- Sees a video or post showing an AI responding to a WhatsApp inquiry at 4am and closing a sale while the owner sleeps
- Realizes he's losing money by being the bottleneck

### Objections
- "¿Puedo pagarlo? ¿Cuánto sale?"
- "¿Es difícil de configurar?"
- "¿Y si el AI responde mal?"

### Best Marketing Angle
**"While You Sleep"** — Show the 4am WhatsApp notification. Make it visceral and relatable.

### Preferred Channels
- Instagram Reels / TikTok (short, punchy)
- WhatsApp broadcast from a peer or influencer
- YouTube Shorts

## Persona 2: "La Tienda Chica" — Sebastián

**Role:** Owner of a small Apple products shop
**Size:** 3-10 employees, 1-2 physical locations
**Location:** Commercial zone, GBA or interior cities (Córdoba, Rosario, Mendoza)
**Age:** 30-45
**Channel:** Has physical store + Instagram + MercadoLibre. Maybe a website.

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Has an employee (or himself) updating stock in Excel after every sale
- Gets angry when a customer calls asking for a product that's already sold
- Struggles to keep pricing updated across all channels when the dollar moves
- Has no visibility into which products generate the most margin
- Runs promotions based on gut feeling, not data

### Pain Points
- "El Excel ya no da más, tenemos 300 SKUs"
- "Me entero que se acabó el stock cuando el cliente ya lo compró"
- "No sé si estoy ganando o perdiendo en cada producto"
- "Cuando el dólar sube, tardo horas en actualizar todos los precios"

### Trigger to Buy Oragon
- Sees a demo of the inventory updating in real-time with USD conversion
- Gets shown the dashboard with margin analytics per product

### Objections
- "¿Puedo migrar mi Excel viejo?"
- "¿Funciona con mi sistema actual?"
- "¿Cuánto tiempo lleva implementarlo?"

### Best Marketing Angle
**"Death of Excel"** — Show the migration from chaotic spreadsheet to clean real-time dashboard. Fast, elegant, dramatic.

### Preferred Channels
- LinkedIn (decision-maker mode)
- YouTube (longer explainers / demos)
- Google Search ads ("sistema para reventa apple argentina")
- Cold email outreach

## Persona 3: "El Distribuidor" — Martín

**Role:** Mid-size distributor or multi-location reseller
**Size:** 10-50 employees, 2-5 locations or online-heavy operation
**Location:** CABA, or national distribution
**Age:** 35-55
**Channel:** Has own e-commerce, MercadoLibre Platinum, corporate sales team

### Day-to-Day Reality
- Has multiple people managing inventory across locations — reconciliation is a nightmare
- Needs consolidated reporting across all channels: who sold what, where, at what margin
- Wants to give his sales team a tool to close deals faster
- Has tried other SaaS tools that weren't built for Argentina (no ARS support, no MercadoLibre integration)
- Wants AI automation but is skeptical of tools that don't understand the local market

### Pain Points
- "Tenemos ventas en 3 sucursales y no sé qué pasa en tiempo real"
- "Ningún sistema SaaS fue hecho para Argentina — no entienden el tema del dólar"
- "Mi equipo de ventas no tiene visibilidad del stock cuando está con un cliente"
- "Necesito reportes pero armarlos lleva días"

### Trigger to Buy Oragon
- Sees consolidated multi-channel analytics dashboard demo
- Talks to another distributor who already uses Oragon

### Objections
- "¿Tiene API para integrar con nuestros sistemas?"
- "¿Cómo manejan la seguridad de mis datos?"
- "¿Qué pasa si necesito algo a medida?"

### Best Marketing Angle
**"Ultimate CRM"** — Show the unified dashboard. Emphasize that it was built for Argentina: ARS pricing, USD sync, local compliance. Peer testimonials carry the most weight.

### Preferred Channels
- LinkedIn (direct outreach + content)
- Cold email (personalized, data-backed)
- Industry WhatsApp groups
- Referrals from existing customers
